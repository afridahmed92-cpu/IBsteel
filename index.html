Welcome to IB Steels & Metals

Every project is completed with a focus on strength, safety, clean finishing and customer satisfaction.

About Us

About IB Steels & Metals

IB Steels & Metals was established in 2018 with a simple purpose: to provide reliable steel materials and dependable workmanship to construction companies and property owners.

The company initially focused on supplying steel products to construction businesses. With growing experience, strong customer relationships and increasing demand, IB Steels & Metals expanded into complete project execution.

Founder Story

Meet Our Founder - Abdul Ahad

The journey of Abdul Ahad, founder of IB Steels & Metals, began far away from his hometown. He previously worked in Dubai, and later explored opportunities in Oman, but his desire to return to his roots and create his own business remained strong.

After returning to India, Abdul Ahad recognised the growing potential of the steel industry. He began by supplying steel materials to construction companies. With dedication, practical experience and customer trust, the business gradually expanded.

What started as a steel supply business has now grown into a complete steel and metal-work company. Since 2018, IB Steels & Metals has successfully completed more than 500 projects.

Rooted in Moodbidri, Abdul Ahad takes pride in serving his community and building a business based on trust and hard work. Beyond business, he is also a proud father to a lovely daughter, one of his greatest sources of motivation.

Alhamdulillah, the journey continues - one strong project at a time.
